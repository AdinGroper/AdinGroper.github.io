My Story

Growing up, I loved the concept of travel. Though I'd been out of the country a few times as a toddler, I can't say I remember much of those trips. It wasn't until I started middle school that I can say I remember the first trip I took outside my home state of Florida: a summer visit to Washington D.C. with my dad.

Every summer after, I'd visit someplace new. It started out with domestic trips, visiting cities within the U.S., and soon expanded internationally aboard a cruise every few years. While I was in college, I had the opportunity to study in France as an exchange student for a semester. During those six months, my love for travel flourished. I couldn't get enough of it! I got to visit new places across 17 different countries, enjoying new experiences, embracing new cultures, and sharing these adventures with friends along the way.

In such a short time, I quickly learned just how big this world truly is. There are so many different people, places, and cultures that exist, and yet despite these differences, we all share one thing in common: our humanity. My dream in life is to experience as much of the world as I can, keeping an open mind and learning everything there is to know about the people and history of this planet. There's just so much I can't wait to explore.

This website serves as a place for me to log my adventures, share photos and experiences from my travels, and keep the memories alive long after they've happened.

Currently I am based in the U.S. out of Boston, Massachusetts, where I work as an aerospace engineer. When I'm not at work, I love exploring new and exciting places around the world. Embrace adventure with me as I share my travels near and abroad.

I hope you enjoy!

P.S. If you happen to stumble across this page on your own, reach out and let me know how you got here!
