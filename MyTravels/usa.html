United States

The United States is a country in North America made up of 50 states, spanning from the Atlantic coast in the east to the Pacific coast in the west, with Alaska in the far northwest and Hawaii in the central Pacific. It is one of the most geographically and culturally diverse countries in the world, and home to some of the most iconic cities, landscapes, and landmarks on the planet.
